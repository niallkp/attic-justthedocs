Apache Any23

Any23 retired in June 2023 and the move to the Attic was completed in October 2023.

The mission of Any23 (Anything to Triples) was the creation and maintenance of software related to automatic crawling, parsing, analyzing, producing, validating and converting RDF (Resource Description Framework) data.
